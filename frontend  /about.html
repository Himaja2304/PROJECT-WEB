Project Goal

Your project focuses on personalized color recommendations. The goal is to extract colors from an uploaded image (primarily focusing on a person’s eyeball color, skin color, and hair color) and use this data to suggest a tailored color palette that best suits the person.

Key Features

  • Image Upload and Processing: Users can upload a close-up photo of themselves. Extract pixel data from specific areas of the image:
    • Eyeball Color: Analyze eye region for dominant color.
    • Skin Tone: Detect and analyze skin tone.
    • Hair Color: Extract the primary hair color.
  • Personalized Color Suggestions: Based on the extracted colors, generate a customized color palette. Suggestions will follow color theory principles (e.g., complementary or contrasting colors) to enhance the user’s natural features.
  • Interactive Output: Display detected eye, skin, and hair colors visually. Show a palette of recommended colors with their HEX or RGB values. Allow users to save or share the generated palette.
  • Enhanced Features (Optional):
    • Simulate color palettes for specific occasions (formal, casual, etc.).
    • Offer accessibility checks or colorblind-friendly suggestions.

How the Project Works

Image Processing:

Use image processing techniques (like OpenCV or libraries such as color-thief.js) to extract dominant colors from specific facial regions. Algorithms will focus on identifying colors in the eye, skin, and hair regions.

Color Palette Generation:

Generate a palette of colors that complement the extracted features. Apply color harmony rules (e.g., analogous, triadic, or split-complementary).

User Experience:

A simple and responsive homepage where users can:

  • Upload an image.
  • View extracted colors and suggested palettes.
  • Save, download, or share the palette.

Technology Stack

  • Front-end: HTML, CSS, JavaScript.
  • Back-end : Store user data or saved palettes in a database and combinations.
